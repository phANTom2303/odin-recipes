Cheesy Chilla Bites

A cheesy spin on an otherwise boring breakfast meal, making it yummy.

Ingredients

  • Besan - 250gm
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 TSP
  • Red chilli powder - 1tsp
  • Garam masala - 1/2 TSP
  • Chopped capsicum - 1tbs
  • Chopped onion - 1tbs
  • Chopped tomatoes - 1tbs
  • Chopped green chillies - 2
  • Chopped coriander leaves - 1tbs
  • Baking soda - 1/4 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Take a bowl, add besan and mix all the spices well.
  2. Add water and make batter.
  3. Add all the vegetables and mix well.
  4. Add baking soda.
  5. Spread the batter on heated tawa and roast from both sides using oil.
  6. Spread grated cheese on it and roll it and cut into bite size.
