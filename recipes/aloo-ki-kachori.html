Aloo Ki Kachori

This is a favourite dry snack that is the perfect match with monsoon season.

Ingredients

  • Refined flour - 2 cups
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp
  • Carrom seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Oil - 1/4 cup
  • Potato - 250gm ( boiled & mashed)
  • Oil - 1 tbs
  • Ginger - 1 tsp grated
  • Green chillies - 2 ( finely chopped)
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Cumin powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Salt -1/2 tsp
  • Red chilli powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Garam masala -1/2 tsp
  • Dry mango powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander leaves (optional)- 2 tbs
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a bowl add 2 cups of refined flour salt & oil, mix well.
  2. Add water to the mix and knead into a soft dough. Rest the dough for 30min.
  3. Take a pan heat oil add ginger, green chillies & all the dry spices. Saut√© for 1- 2 min.
  4. Add boiled potato (mashed) to spices and roast for 5 min. Keep stuffing aside to cool down.
  5. From dough cut out small balls.
  6. Roll the ball and add small amount of stuffing and seal it properly.
  7. Heat the oil and deep fry all the kachori in medium flame for 7-8 min till it turns golden brown and crispy.
